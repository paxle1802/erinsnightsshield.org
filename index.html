Our Mission

Quiet, faithful funding for the work of sheltering children.

Erin's Night's Shield Foundation is a 501(c)(3) supporting organization established in 2017 to provide sustainable, long-term funding for The Night's Shield Children's Shelter and its homeless youth programs — a 24-hour emergency shelter serving unhoused young people ages 11 to 23, including pregnant and parenting youth and LGBTQ+ youth, across 17 counties of southern Illinois.

Founded in loving memory of Erin Davis Howell, we raise funds through community events, sponsorship partnerships, and direct gifts so that the work entrusted to us — sheltering children, supporting parenting youth, and standing with families in crisis — can plan and serve with confidence for generations to come.

A gift to the foundation is, in its essence, an investment in possibility: the conviction that no child in our region should face a night without shelter. Every gift becomes a seed for community impact that endures long beyond the moment it is given.

Where Your Gift Goes

Daily needs. Lasting dignity.

Your support funds the daily essentials that turn a shelter bed into a place of dignity, safety, and possibility.

Education

School & supplies

Backpacks, notebooks, school clothes, tutoring support, and fees that let young people walk into class without being marked by their circumstances.

Daily care

Food, clothing & hygiene

Meals, seasonal clothing, toiletries, and the steady supply of basics that turn a shelter bed into a place of dignity and safety.

Stability

Medical & parenting

Medical co-pays, prescriptions, parenting classes for pregnant and parenting youth, and the wraparound support families need to stay safe.

Our Signature Event

Bocce Fun Day, Herrin, Illinois.

A day of friendly competition, food, raffle prizes, and fellowship — and the cornerstone of our annual fundraising.

Annual tradition · HerrinFesta Italiana Bocce Complex

Play. Sponsor. Show up.

Each fall we gather at the historic Herrin Bocce Courts for our signature fundraiser. Friendly, family-friendly, and the dollars raised at the courts translate directly into shelter beds, school supplies, and warm meals for unhoused youth.

Individual, team, court, and tournament sponsorships available each season. Contact the foundation for the next event date.

Our Stewardship

Lean. Faithful. Verifiable.

Erin's Night's Shield Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) public charity. Our annual Form 990-EZ filings are public record and may be verified through the IRS Tax Exempt Organization Search or ProPublica's Nonprofit Explorer using our EIN 81-4410406.

We operate with the modesty appropriate to a small supporting organization: a lean administrative footprint, volunteer leadership, and a steady commitment to keeping costs low so that more of every gift reaches the children the mission is given to serve.
