Our Mission

Faithful funding for the work of sheltering children.

Erin's Night's Shield Foundation is a 501(c)(3) supporting organization established in 2017 to provide sustainable, long-term funding for the partner shelter and homeless youth services serving unhoused young people across 17 counties of southern Illinois.

Founded in loving memory of Erin Davis Howell, we raise funds through community events, sponsorship partnerships, and direct gifts so that the work entrusted to us — sheltering children, supporting parenting youth, and standing with families in crisis — can plan and serve with confidence for generations to come.

A gift to the foundation is, in its essence, an act of hope: the conviction that what is good and faithful today will continue to bear fruit long after we have departed. Every gift becomes a seed for community impact that endures.

What Your Gift Becomes

One gift. Lasting impact.

P

Sustainable Support

Annual fundraising — anchored by our Bocce Fun Day tradition — generates predictable, year-after-year funding so that The Night's Shield can plan its programs with confidence rather than scrambling year to year.

S

Faithful Stewardship

We operate as a small supporting organization with a lean administrative footprint. Volunteer-led, professionally governed, and committed to keeping costs low so that more of every gift reaches the children the mission is given to serve.

C

Community Impact

Funds flow to the 24-hour shelter, homeless youth program, case management, advocacy, transitional living, and community outreach — meeting real, immediate needs while building lasting capacity for the years ahead.

Where the Funding Goes

Daily needs. Lasting dignity.

Your gifts fund the daily essentials that homeless youth in southern Illinois deserve.

E

Education & School Supplies

Backpacks, notebooks, school clothes, tutoring support, and fees that let young people walk into a classroom without being marked by their circumstances.

H

Food, Clothing & Hygiene

Meals, seasonal clothing, toiletries, and the steady supply of basics that turn a shelter bed into a place of dignity.

M

Medical & Parenting Support

Medical co-pays, prescriptions, parenting classes for pregnant and parenting youth, and the wraparound support families need to stay safe and stable.

Annual Tradition · Herrin, Illinois

Bocce Fun Day

Each fall, we gather at the HerrinFesta Italiana Bocce Complex for our signature fundraiser — a day of friendly competition, raffle prizes, food, and fellowship that has become a cherished tradition for sponsors, players, and the children we serve.

Individual play, team registration, and court sponsorship opportunities are available each year. Contact us for the current season's date and registration details.

Ways to Give

Every gift, large or small, builds the shield.

Please reach out to discuss the option that best reflects your intentions. We're a small organization — your gift goes a long way.

Outright Gift

A direct gift of cash, check, or appreciated securities is the simplest way to support the foundation. Outright gifts may be eligible for an income tax deduction in the year they are made. Make checks payable to Erin's Night's Shield Foundation.

Event Sponsorship

Sponsor a Bocce Fun Day court ($250), tournament ($1,000), or team ($500). Individual or business sponsorships start at $150. Sponsorship is a meaningful way to align your name or business with the work of sheltering children.

Bequest in Will

Naming the foundation as a beneficiary in your will or living trust is a meaningful way to leave a lasting legacy of compassion without affecting your present resources. Please reach out to discuss bequest language.

IRA Charitable Rollover

Donors aged 70½ or older may make a Qualified Charitable Distribution from a traditional IRA. The transfer can satisfy required minimum distributions while supporting the foundation tax-efficiently.

Donor-Advised Fund

If you maintain a donor-advised fund through a community foundation or financial institution, you may recommend a grant to Erin's Night's Shield Foundation using our EIN 81-4410406.

Play or Volunteer

Register for Bocce Fun Day as a $30 individual or $120 team. Or volunteer at registration, raffle service, or event setup. Time is its own form of generosity.

Our Stewardship

Lean. Faithful. Verifiable.

Erin's Night's Shield Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) public charity. Our annual Form 990-EZ filings are public record and may be verified through the IRS Tax Exempt Organization Search or ProPublica's Nonprofit Explorer using our EIN 81-4410406.

We operate with the modesty appropriate to a small supporting organization: a lean administrative footprint, volunteer leadership, and a steady commitment to keeping costs low so that more of every gift reaches the children the mission is given to serve.
